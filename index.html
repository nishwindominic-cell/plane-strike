🔒 PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: March 2026

📋 1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This game operates entirely locally on your device and does not collect any personal information through servers or third-party services. All data is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage.

Local Storage Data Includes:

  • ✅ Game progress (level, stage, score, in-game dollars)
  • ✅ Settings preferences (sound, volume, control options)
  • ✅ Achievements and unlocked planes
  • ✅ Player statistics (kills, landings, crashes, win rate)
  • ✅ Pilot information (name, age, country, experience)
  • ✅ Home coins balance and transaction history
  • ✅ Mystery boxes count and collection history
  • ✅ Free spins remaining and cooldown timers
  • ✅ Daily reward timestamps
  • ✅ Notification log with timestamps
  • ✅ High score

🔐 2. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using the browser's localStorage system. This means:

  • 🚫 No data is ever transmitted to any server
  • 🚫 No cookies are used for tracking
  • 🚫 No analytics or advertising networks are integrated
  • ✅ Your data stays on your device and is only accessible by you
  • ✅ Data persists between gaming sessions until you clear your browser data

👤 3. Pilot Information

The pilot information you provide (name, age, country, experience) is stored locally and used only for:

  • Personalizing your gaming experience with welcome messages
  • Tracking your pilot statistics and achievements
  • Creating a unique pilot profile that saves your progress

This information is never shared with any third party and cannot be accessed by anyone else.

🎮 4. Game Features & Data Usage

Various game features use local storage to enhance your experience:

  • Home Coins: Your coin balance is saved locally and used to unlock planes
  • Mystery Boxes: Box counts are saved and persist between sessions
  • Achievements: Progress and completed achievements are tracked locally
  • Plane Unlocks: Purchased planes remain unlocked permanently
  • Statistics: Your kill counts, landings, and other stats are preserved
  • Notification Log: All game notifications are saved for your review
  • High Score: Your highest score is saved permanently

🧒 5. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Since all data is stored locally and no online accounts are required, there is no risk of children's data being exposed online.

🔄 6. Data Deletion & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Reset Statistics: Use the "Reset Statistics" button in Pilot Profile to clear your gameplay stats
  • Sign Out: Use the "SIGN OUT" button in Pilot Data to clear your pilot information
  • Clear Notifications: Use the "CLEAR ALL" button in the notification log
  • Browser Data: Clearing your browser's localStorage will remove all game data

🌐 7. Third-Party Services

This game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking services, or external APIs. No data is shared with any external parties. The game functions completely offline once loaded.

📱 8. Mobile Devices

On mobile devices, the game requests landscape orientation for optimal gameplay. No device identifiers, location data, or personal information is accessed or stored.

🔄 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the game or for clarification. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the game constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📧 10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us through the game's official support channel. We are committed to addressing your inquiries promptly.
